We were tired of

It's an open secret in the HVAC industry. The technician isn't paid to fix your AC. They are paid to find "opportunities."

We worked for those big companies. We sat in the morning meetings where managers screamed about "average ticket value." We were told to sell, sell, sell.

So we quit.

Brimmer was born with one rule: Technicians fix. Salesmen sell. We only hire technicians.
